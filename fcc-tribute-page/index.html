"Pig-Pen"

The Peanuts character who wasn't afraid of getting his hands dirty
"Pig-Pen" standing in a cloud of dirt, smiling passively.

    Things "Pig-Pen" is known for:

  • Being perpetually dirty
  • When cleaned, becoming dirty again instantly
  • There are unconfirmed reports of a relationship developing between "Pig-Pen" and Peppermint Patty. There are rumors that Charlie Brown stole her from him.
  • "Pig Pen" first appeared in the Peanuts comic strip on July 13, 1954.
  • His last appearance in the comic strip was in 1999. There is no information about his current whereabouts.
  • Though there are some claims that the dirty around "Pig-Pen" may have historical significance, it is mostly likely just midwestern topsoil.

""Pigpen" is a human soil bank who raises a cloud of dust on a perfectly clean street and passes out gum drops that are invariably black."

-- Charles M. Schulz on "Pig-Pen"
